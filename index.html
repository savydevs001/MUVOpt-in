SMS Privacy & Opt‑In Policy – MUV Ride

Last updated: 8 August 2025

1. Purpose of SMS Messaging

When users sign up or reset their password, MUV Ride requests their mobile number to send a One‑Time Password (OTP) via SMS for authentication. These messages are strictly transactional and used for account verification only.

2. How the Sign‑Up Flow Works

This documentation illustrates how users consent to SMS messages during the sign‑up process:

Step 1: Enter Details and Phone Number

Step 2: User Chooses OTP Delivery Method

When the popup displays, users explicitly choose between “Phone (SMS)” or “Email” for receiving the OTP. Choosing “Phone” functions as an explicit opt‑in to receive SMS messages for verification.

3. SMS Opt‑In Agreement

If the user selects Phone, they agree to:

  • Receive automated SMS messages from MUV Ride for authentication only.
  • “Msg & data rates may apply.”
  • Reply STOP to opt‑out at any time. Once opted out, SMS delivery is disabled until re‑opt‑in.
  • Consent is recorded with timestamp, phone number, and user ID.

4. Opt‑Out & Support

Although OTPs are transactional, users may reply STOP to any SMS to stop receiving OTPs. They may also reply HELP for support or contact us via support@muvrides.com.

5. Data Use & Retention

We collect mobile numbers solely to send and verify OTPs. We do not use phone numbers for marketing or share them, except with Twilio or similar providers involved in delivery. Phone numbers are kept only as long as needed for account and security purposes.
